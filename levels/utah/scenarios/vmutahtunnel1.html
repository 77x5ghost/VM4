Escape from the Tunnel!


An earthquake destroyed the tunnel , and blocked all escape routes , except one.

Just follow the available routes and hope to find a way out of there.


But beware

The right side of the tunnel , (the one you're starting in) is damaged. Watch out for rocks.


Tips & Hints


- Don't forget to turn on your lights

- Don't slow down too late before you enter a turn. They are closer than they appear.
